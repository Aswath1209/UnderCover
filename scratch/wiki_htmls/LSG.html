The 2026 season was the 5th season for the Indian Premier League (IPL) cricket franchise Lucknow Super Giants. They were one of the ten teams that competed in the 2026 IPL.

Lucknow Super Giants were the first team to be eliminated from the 2026 playoffs race, with their elimination being confirmed from Royal Challengers Bengaluru's 2-wicket victory over Mumbai Indians on 10 May, 2026 in Raipur. They won only four of their fourteen games of the league stage.
